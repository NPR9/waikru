FIGHT PLAN
Your 9-month camp structure
Camp Phases — 9 Month Structure
1
Months 1–3 · Foundation
Build base technique. Jab, cross, teep, roundhouse. Heavy conditioning. 3–4 sessions/week. Focus: getting kicks to match hand quality. You're here now.
2
Months 4–5 · Combination Building
Link techniques. Build 3–5 signature combos. Start developing your style. Increase sparring frequency. Work reaction drills daily.
3
Months 6–7 · Pressure Testing
More sparring. Identify your fighting style. Work on timing under pressure. Begin weight management seriously.
4
Month 8 · Pre-Camp Sharpening
Reduce volume, increase intensity. Sharpen your 3–4 best weapons. Cut to fight weight. Mental prep begins.
5
Month 9 · Fight Week
Taper training. Light technical work only. Full weight cut. Rest. Visualise. Trust your training.
